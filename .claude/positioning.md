# StorageAds — Canonical Positioning

> **This file is the single source of truth for what StorageAds is and how we sell it.** Every copy-generation surface, prompt, and voice doc defers to the hierarchy below. When `BRAND_DOCTRINE.md`, `CREATIVE.md`, `.claude/copy-voice.md`, the `operator-copy` skill, or any ad/landing/email prompt conflicts with this file on *message hierarchy or differentiation*, this file wins. (Those docs still own their domains: aesthetics, voice mechanics, register separation. This file owns the positioning.)
>
> Customer register is the default. Pitch register (`.claude/pitch-voice.md`) inverts some word rules for investors and acquirers, but it still leads with the full system, never with attribution alone.

## HERO

StorageAds is marketing infrastructure that turns ad spend into move-ins for independent storage operators — the same machine Public Storage, Extra Space, and U-Haul run on, productized for the operators who don't have it.

## CORE PROMISE

More move-ins, higher occupancy, more revenue, by fixing the entire customer acquisition funnel — not just running ads. A tenant's lifetime value is thousands of dollars; the system exists to capture demand that currently goes uncaptured because no one in the industry markets right.

## FUNNEL PILLARS — this is the product. Weight copy here.

1. **Market mapping** — competitors, their pricing, their reviews, your trade area.
2. **Meta + Google paid acquisition** — Meta to reach renters before they search, Google to capture the ones already looking, retargeting to bring back leavers.
3. **Per-ad landing pages with the storEDGE rental flow embedded** — the renter books on your branded page, the reservation lands in your system.
4. **Reservation-to-move-in conversion optimization.**
5. **Facility audit, ancillary revenue streams, tax advantages** — checking every box that lifts the bottom line toward 100% occupancy.
6. **Self-serve or fully managed** — same platform either way.

## ATTRIBUTION — DEMOTED ROLE

Attribution is the measurement layer underneath the funnel, not a pitch. It exists so optimization is real: every move-in tied back to the creative that produced it, A/B tests scored by move-ins instead of clicks, wasted spend killed fast. It is proof the system works, never the reason to buy.

---

## RULES FOR ATTRIBUTION MENTIONS

These apply to all customer-facing copy and to every prompt that generates it.

1. **Never the lede, headline, or primary differentiator.** Attribution does not open a page, a section, an email, or an ad.
2. **Cap it to a supporting line** inside a funnel or optimization section. One line, in service of the outcome.
3. **Ban the jargon in customer-facing copy.** No "attribution," "closed-loop," "closed-loop attribution," "ad-to-move-in attribution," "full-funnel attribution," "cost per reservation," or "cost per move-in" used as pitch language. ("cost per move-in," "attribution," "dashboard," "ad platforms" remain acceptable on deep product pages, docs, and the help center — never on a hero, landing, ad, or email.)
4. **Translate to operator outcomes instead:**

| Don't say (mechanism) | Say (operator outcome) |
|---|---|
| "closed-loop / full-funnel attribution" | "you'll know exactly which ads brought paying tenants" |
| "cost-per-move-in attribution" | "every dollar tracked to a move-in, so we cut what doesn't work" |
| "we A/B test and optimize the funnel" | "we test against move-ins, not clicks" |
| "single-domain attribution, no tracking loss" | "nothing falls through the cracks between the ad and the rented unit" |

## BANNED FRAMING — differentiation

- **No "only ones who do X right" framing of any kind.** We do not own a measurement term and we do not win by claiming we're the only ones who track correctly. Drop "the only," "nobody else," "no one else does this," and every variant when the X is a measurement or attribution capability.
- **Differentiate on the full system and the operator-built angle, not on owning a measurement term.** What separates StorageAds: it is the whole acquisition machine the REITs run, built by an operator who had the same problem, plugged in for operators who don't have it. The competitor we displace is the operator's inaction, not another vendor's tracking.
