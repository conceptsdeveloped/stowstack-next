How the Product Works

A plain-English map of StorageAds — software that does the marketing for self-storage facilities. No technical knowledge needed. Read it top to bottom: it follows the same path a real visitor or customer takes.

🏢 Think of it like a building. The front door & lobby is the public website. The security desk checks who you are and where you're allowed to go. Behind that are the working rooms where the actual jobs get done. Downstairs is the filing room where everything is stored. And a night crew quietly works around the clock while everyone sleeps.
1

The front doors — who shows up

Four different kinds of people use StorageAds, and each gets their own entrance and their own view.

🌐The public

Storage owners browsing the website, reading the blog, or trying the free marketing "audit" tool. This is how new customers first find us.

🛠️Us (the founders)

Blake & Angelo. A private control room to manage every facility, campaign, and customer.

👤Customers

Paying facility operators. Their own dashboard to see campaigns, bills, and reports.

🤝Partners

Resellers and referrers who bring us business and manage their own clients.

↓
2

The security desk — checking everyone in

Before anyone gets to do anything, the system checks who they are and what they're allowed to touch. Each of the four groups above carries a different kind of "badge," and the desk also blocks anything that looks fake or unsafe.

🪪Different keys for different people

Founders, customers, and partners each log in their own way and only see their own stuff.

🛡️Safety checks

Blocks fake or harmful requests and keeps each visitor's data walled off from everyone else's.

🚨Alarm system

If something breaks, it's flagged instantly so we know exactly where to look.

The working rooms — everything the product actually does

This is the heart of it, and it's big. Below is the full list, organized into the "departments" inside the building. Each line is one thing the software can do, in plain English. Items marked ANGELO are the ad-platform and AI video/image pieces Angelo owns.

🎯

Department 1 — Find & win new customers

the sales funnel

Everything that turns a storage owner who's never heard of us into a paying customer. This is the front of the whole business.

›Free instant audit. An owner types their facility name and instantly gets a letter-grade scorecard of their online presence (Google rating, reviews, photos, website, phone).
›Shareable audit report. Each audit lives at its own web link with "money you're leaving on the table" math, used as the hook on a sales call.
›Sample audit. A ready-made example so a prospect can see the deliverable without entering their own facility.
›Deep diagnostic. A longer 5-step questionnaire that produces a full AI-written facility report for more serious prospects.
›Revenue / ROAS calculator. Owner enters units, occupancy, and budget and sees estimated move-ins, added revenue, and return on ad spend.
›"Cost of doing nothing" timeline. A month-by-month picture of what six months of inaction costs while a competitor advertises.
›Comparison pages. "StorageAds vs. [competitor]" pages that handle objections and pull in search traffic.
›Case studies. Real before-and-after results pages that build proof.
›Blog & operator notes. File-based articles, an RSS feed, and short first-person essays that bring in organic visitors.
›Help center, pricing, demo & overview pages. The standard supporting marketing pages and an interactive product demo used on live sales calls.
›Self-serve signup. A "start your 14-day free trial" account-creation flow.
›Sticky mobile button & exit-intent popup. Catch-the-visitor prompts tuned for phone and Facebook traffic before they leave.
🧲

Department 2 — Capture, score & chase leads

never lose a lead

Once someone shows interest, this department makes sure they're captured, ranked, and followed up with automatically.

›Lead capture. Saves every prospect from the audit, popups, and landing-page forms in one place.
›Half-finished form rescue. Even captures people who start a form but don't finish, so they aren't lost.
›Win-back console. A screen for chasing dropped or cold leads back into the pipeline.
›Lead scoring. Automatically ranks leads by how promising they are so sales focuses on the hottest first.
›Lead matching. Recognizes when a new lead is really an existing contact (by phone, email, or name) and merges them.
›Drip & nurture sequences. Pre-written multi-step email/text follow-ups that fire on a schedule ("Day 2," "Hour 1," etc.).
›AI marketing plan. Generates a tailored per-facility marketing plan to use as a sales/onboarding leave-behind.
›Lead pipeline & board. A list view and a drag-and-drop board for moving leads through sales stages.
›Lead export. Download the whole lead list as a spreadsheet.
🌐

Department 3 — Landing pages & funnels

where ad clicks land

The custom web pages that ad traffic lands on, plus the automated path from "clicked an ad" to "filled out a form."

›Landing page builder. Build custom, section-by-section ad landing pages per facility, with templates and an image picker.
›AI page generator. Auto-writes a landing page from scratch for a facility or ad.
›Funnel builder. Generates a complete funnel — landing page plus the follow-up email steps after someone converts.
›Built-in reservation widget. Pages can embed the facility's storEDGE booking so renters can reserve right there.
›Funnel metrics. Shows how many people make it through each stage of the funnel.
🎨

Department 4 — Make the ad creative ANGELO

AI design studio

The AI "creative studio" that produces the words, pictures, and videos for ads — automatically and on-brand.

›Ad copy writer. Generates ready-to-run Facebook/Instagram ad text in several angles (urgency, convenience, social proof, lifestyle).
›Google ad & keyword writer. Writes Google search ads and suggests 25–35 storage keywords grouped by theme.
›Full creative bundle. One click produces a matched set: Facebook ad, Google ad, landing-page copy, and a 4-email sequence.
›AI image generator. Creates ad images from 10 templates (hero, lifestyle, before/after, seasonal, story background, etc.).
›Stock image library. A curated library of storage/moving photos to drop into ads.
›AI video generator. Makes short marketing videos from 7 templates and 11 cinematic styles.
›TikTok slideshow maker. Builds vertical TikTok-style slideshow videos with zoom/pan effects from facility photos.
›Organic social post writer. Writes everyday social posts, including bulk batches tied to a seasonal calendar (college move-in, holidays, spring cleaning).
›Ad mockup preview. Shows the finished ad exactly as it'll look in a Facebook feed or Google display slot before publishing.
›Brand & style memory. Upload reference images and the system learns a facility's visual style and applies it to everything it makes.
📣

Department 5 — Publish & manage the ads ANGELO

push it live

Taking finished ads live on the real platforms, then tracking the money and results that come back.

›One-click publishing. Pushes a finished ad live to Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads, or TikTok directly.
›Organic social publishing. Posts directly to Facebook, Instagram, and the Google listing on a schedule.
›Platform connections. Securely links each facility's Facebook/Google/TikTok ad accounts.
›Audience syncing. Builds custom and "lookalike" ad audiences from the facility's tenant list (privacy-hashed).
›Conversion tracking. Reports leads and move-ins back to Facebook and Google so their systems optimize the ads.
›Spend tracking & attribution. Tracks daily ad spend and ties it to which specific ad produced each click, lead, and move-in.
›Campaign alerts. Flags problems automatically ("cost per lead too high," "no campaign launched 14 days after signing").
›Self-improving creative. Studies which ads worked and feeds those lessons back into future ad writing.
📊

Department 6 — Market & money intelligence

the analyst

The "analyst" that studies each facility's numbers, the competition, and the local market, then tells the owner exactly where money is being lost or made.

›Occupancy intelligence. Shows how full a facility really is and why occupied units still aren't capturing full revenue.
›12-month occupancy forecast. Projects future occupancy and contrasts "with ads" vs. "without ads."
›Revenue health score & analytics. A weighted health score plus a breakdown of where revenue leaks out.
›Revenue-loss analyzer. Puts a dollar figure on money bleeding from six causes (empty units, underpricing, missed leads, competitors, churn, discounts) and the payback from fixing it.
›Rate-increase finder (ECRI). Spots long-term tenants paying below market and suggests a new rate and the revenue lift.
›Rate-increase safety scoring. Scores how likely each tenant is to leave if their rate goes up, so increases target the safest ones.
›NOI / value-delivered report. A periodic snapshot showing the net profit StorageAds added versus its fee.
›Churn prediction. Flags which tenants are likely to move out, why, and what to do to keep them.
›Retention tracking. Records whether the save-the-tenant efforts actually worked.
›Upsell finder. Surfaces expansion opportunities (add insurance, autopay, bigger unit, annual lease) with the dollar upside.
›Move-out win-back. Automatically re-markets to former tenants and tracks who comes back.
›Competitor & market scan. Maps nearby competitors, their pricing/promos, local demographics, and demand drivers (apartments, colleges, military bases).
›Site-map reader. Looks at a storEDGE site-map screenshot and reads every unit's status (occupied, vacant, past-due, reserved).
›Seasonal context. Tells the owner whether they're in peak, shoulder, or slow season and what to do about it.
⭐

Department 7 — Google reviews & local listing

reputation

Manages each facility's all-important Google Business Profile — the listing people see when they search "storage near me."

›Google posts. Writes and publishes Google updates/offers/events, grounded in real data (only advertising units that are actually vacant).
›Review management. Pulls in Google reviews and AI-drafts tone-appropriate replies to approve and publish.
›Auto-respond settings. Choose to auto-reply to reviews above a chosen star rating, in a chosen tone.
›Q&A management. Answers the questions people post on the listing, and pre-seeds common storage questions.
›Listing sync. Pushes correct hours and photos to Google and flags when the listing has drifted from reality.
›Listing insights. Reports how many people saw, clicked, called, or asked directions from the listing.
›Review requests. Emails or texts recent move-ins a direct link asking for a Google review.
📋

Department 8 — Facility records & data uploads

the data feed

How each facility's real numbers get into the system, since all the intelligence above depends on it. (Note: data comes in by manual upload today — there's no live automatic connection to storEDGE yet.)

›Report upload. Owners (or the team) upload facility management reports as spreadsheets.
›storEDGE report import. Reads specific storEDGE reports — rent roll, occupancy, aging, revenue, length-of-stay — into organized tables.
›Smart column matching. Automatically figures out which spreadsheet columns are which, even when formats differ.
›Unit mix & specials. Tracks each facility's unit types, counts, rates, and active promotions.
›Upload review queue. A founder-side queue to review and approve uploaded reports before they go live.
👤

Department 9 — The customer's own dashboard

what clients see

The self-service portal a paying facility operator logs into (by email + a code) to see their results and manage their account.

›Results dashboard. Leads and move-ins over the last 90 days, plus a monthly move-in goal tracker.
›Campaign performance. Spend, leads, move-ins, cost-per-lead, and return on ad spend over different time ranges.
›Reports & occupancy. Occupancy snapshot, trend chart, unit-mix table, and a downloadable report.
›Reputation view. Their Google rating, review count, and recent reviews.
›Upload reports. Drag-and-drop their management reports for the team to process.
›Two-way messaging. A chat thread with the StorageAds team.
›Billing & invoices. View invoices (ad spend + fee) and update payment method via Stripe.
›Setup wizard. A 6-step onboarding (brand, demographics, unit pricing, competitors, ad preferences, review) that saves and resumes.
›Mobile app feel. Installable on a phone, works offline, with bottom tabs and pull-to-refresh.
🤝

Department 10 — Partner / reseller program

management companies

A separate dashboard for management companies and referral partners who manage many facilities or send us business.

›Multi-facility overview. Portfolio totals across every facility a partner manages.
›Add & manage facilities. Add facilities and drill into each one's occupancy, leads, and campaign history.
›Team management. Invite teammates with roles (Admin/Manager/Viewer).
›Revenue-share program. Earnings, tiers (Bronze→Platinum, 20–35%), next-tier progress, referrals, and payout history.
›Referral engine. Referral codes that auto-issue credits and milestone bonuses as referrals sign up.
›White-label branding. Partners can put their own name/colors/logo on the product.
›Developer tools. API keys and webhooks so partners can plug StorageAds into their own systems.
›Audit log & changelog. A security activity log and a product-updates feed.
💳

Department 11 — Billing, signup & accounts

getting paid

Everything around money coming in and account security.

›Plans & checkout. Per-facility pricing tiers with a 14-day trial, handled through Stripe.
›Subscription syncing. Keeps each account's status (trial, active, past-due, canceled) up to date automatically.
›Invoices. Founder-issued invoices (ad spend + management fee) clients can view and pay.
›Usage limits & feature gates. Each plan unlocks certain features and caps (facilities, landing pages, team size, live ads).
›Security: 2FA, sessions, password reset. Two-factor login, active-session management, and password recovery.
›Privacy & data deletion. A public request flow to delete personal data (GDPR/CCPA), including the Facebook data-deletion callback.
✉️

Department 12 — Messages, reminders & tracking

staying in touch

All the ways the system reaches out to people, and how it knows where every lead and call came from.

›Templated emails. A library of branded sales/service emails (follow-up, audit delivery, proposal, check-in, win-back).
›White-label emails. Emails that carry a partner's branding instead of StorageAds'.
›Text messages. Outbound SMS (with quiet-hours and opt-out rules built in).
›Push notifications. Browser/phone push alerts to customers and partners.
›Notification bell & preferences. An in-app alert center with per-type on/off settings.
›Call tracking. Dedicated phone numbers per page that log and attribute inbound calls.
›Walk-in attribution. A quick counter form asking new tenants "how did you hear about us?" to capture offline sources.
›Link & visit tracking. Trackable short links and heatmap-style page interaction data to see what's working.

Every customer, facility, campaign, and report is stored safely so nothing gets lost and everything can be found again instantly.

🗄️Main records

The big organized database holding customers, facilities, leads, campaigns, and history.

⚡Quick-access memory

Keeps frequently-used info handy so pages load fast.

🖼️File storage

Holds uploaded images, logos, and documents.

📝Written content

The blog articles and guides, kept as simple files.

The night crew — work that happens automatically

About two dozen small automated jobs run on their own schedule, around the clock, with nobody clicking a button. They keep everything fresh and moving.

🔁Follow-ups

Sends the next email or text in a sequence at the right time.

🧮Scoring

Re-checks which customers might cancel and which leads are hot.

📤Reports

Builds and emails the weekly and monthly reports.

🧹Housekeeping

Cleans up old data and keeps things tidy and compliant.

Outside helpers it plugs into

StorageAds doesn't do everything itself — it connects to trusted outside services for specialized work:

🤖 Claude AI — writes audits, copy & plans 💳 Stripe — payments ✉️ Resend — email delivery 📍 Google — facility lookup & listings 🎬 Video & image AI — ad creative 📣 Facebook / Google / TikTok — ad platforms 📅 Cal.com — demo bookings 🚨 Sentry — error alarms
A few things worth knowing: There's no "practice" copy of the site — when we make a change, it goes live to real customers immediately, so changes are handled carefully. The product is pre-launch: the building is nearly finished, and the first real test will be Blake's own storage facilities. The ad-creation and AI-video parts are Angelo's area.
