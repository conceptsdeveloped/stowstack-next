# StorageAds — Complete Feature List, Ideas & Frontier

> Internal-only inventory. Source of truth: `src/lib/ideas-doc.ts` (rendered at `/ideas`, admin-gated). June 2026 · pre-launch.

A plain-language inventory of the StorageAds platform for non-technical readers. Items marked **[ANGELO]** are the ad-platform and AI video/image pieces Angelo owns. Two honest caveats: facility data arrives by **manual upload** today (no live storEDGE connection yet), and SMS/call tracking is built in code but the phone account isn't switched on.

---

## Part 1 · Complete Feature List

Everything the product does, in plain English — grouped into twelve "departments" (~90 features).

### 1. Find & win new customers
- **Free instant audit** — type a facility name, get a letter-grade scorecard of its online presence.
- **Shareable audit report** — each audit gets its own web link with "money left on the table" math for sales calls.
- **Sample audit** — a ready-made example to show prospects.
- **Deep diagnostic** — a 5-step questionnaire that produces a full AI-written facility report.
- **Revenue / ROAS calculator** — estimates move-ins, added revenue, and return on ad spend.
- **"Cost of doing nothing" timeline** — what six months of inaction costs.
- **Comparison pages** — "StorageAds vs. [competitor]" pages for objections and search traffic.
- **Case studies** — real before-and-after results.
- **Blog & operator notes** — articles, RSS feed, and first-person essays for organic traffic.
- **Pricing, demo, help & overview pages** — supporting pages plus a live sales-call demo.
- **Self-serve signup** — a 14-day free-trial account flow.
- **Sticky mobile button & exit popup** — catch-the-visitor prompts for phone/Facebook traffic.

### 2. Capture, score & chase leads
- **Lead capture** — saves every prospect from audits, popups, and forms in one place.
- **Half-finished form rescue** — captures people who start but don't finish a form.
- **Win-back console** — chase dropped or cold leads back into the pipeline.
- **Lead scoring** — auto-ranks leads so sales works the hottest first.
- **Lead matching** — recognizes a new lead as an existing contact and merges them.
- **Drip & nurture sequences** — scheduled multi-step email/text follow-ups.
- **AI marketing plan** — a tailored per-facility plan for sales/onboarding.
- **Pipeline & board** — list and drag-and-drop views of the sales stages.
- **Lead export** — download the whole list as a spreadsheet.

### 3. Landing pages & funnels
- **Landing page builder** — section-by-section ad pages per facility, with templates.
- **AI page generator** — auto-writes a landing page from scratch.
- **Funnel builder** — generates a full funnel: page plus post-conversion emails.
- **Built-in reservation widget** — embed the facility's storEDGE booking on the page.
- **Funnel metrics** — conversion at each funnel stage.

### 4. Make the ad creative [ANGELO]
- **Ad copy writer** — ready-to-run Facebook/Instagram text in several angles.
- **Google ad & keyword writer** — search ads plus 25–35 grouped keywords.
- **Full creative bundle** — a matched FB ad, Google ad, landing page, and email set in one click.
- **AI image generator** — ad images from 10 templates.
- **Stock image library** — curated storage/moving photos.
- **AI video generator** — short videos from 7 templates and 11 styles.
- **TikTok slideshow maker** — vertical slideshow videos with zoom/pan.
- **Organic social writer** — everyday posts, including seasonal bulk batches.
- **Ad mockup preview** — see the ad as it'll look before publishing.
- **Brand & style memory** — learns a facility's visual style and reuses it.

### 5. Publish & manage the ads [ANGELO]
- **One-click publishing** — pushes ads live to Facebook, Instagram, Google, TikTok.
- **Organic social publishing** — posts to FB, IG, and the Google listing on a schedule.
- **Platform connections** — securely links each facility's ad accounts.
- **Audience syncing** — builds custom and lookalike audiences (privacy-hashed).
- **Conversion tracking** — reports leads/move-ins back to FB and Google to optimize ads.
- **Spend tracking & attribution** — ties daily spend to the ad that drove each lead.
- **Campaign alerts** — flags high cost-per-lead or unlaunched campaigns.
- **Self-improving creative** — feeds winning-ad lessons back into future writing.

### 6. Market & money intelligence
- **Occupancy intelligence** — how full a facility really is, and why revenue lags.
- **12-month forecast** — projects occupancy "with ads" vs. "without ads."
- **Revenue health & analytics** — a health score and where revenue leaks.
- **Revenue-loss analyzer** — dollar figures on six causes of lost money + payback.
- **Rate-increase finder (ECRI)** — spots under-priced long-term tenants.
- **Rate-increase safety scoring** — who's least likely to leave if raised.
- **NOI / value report** — net profit StorageAds added vs. its fee.
- **Churn prediction** — which tenants will likely leave, and why.
- **Retention tracking** — whether save efforts actually worked.
- **Upsell finder** — insurance, autopay, bigger unit, annual lease, with upside.
- **Move-out win-back** — re-markets to former tenants automatically.
- **Competitor & market scan** — nearby competitors, pricing, demographics, demand drivers.
- **Site-map reader** — reads unit status from a storEDGE map screenshot.
- **Seasonal context** — peak/shoulder/slow season guidance.

### 7. Google reviews & local listing
- **Google posts** — publishes updates/offers/events grounded in real vacancy data.
- **Review management** — pulls reviews and AI-drafts tone-appropriate replies.
- **Auto-respond settings** — auto-reply above a chosen star rating and tone.
- **Q&A management** — answers listing questions and pre-seeds common ones.
- **Listing sync** — pushes correct hours/photos, flags listing drift.
- **Listing insights** — views, clicks, calls, and direction requests.
- **Review requests** — emails/texts move-ins a direct review link.

### 8. Facility records & data uploads
- **Report upload** — upload facility management reports as spreadsheets.
- **storEDGE report import** — reads rent roll, occupancy, aging, revenue, length-of-stay.
- **Smart column matching** — auto-detects spreadsheet columns across formats.
- **Unit mix & specials** — tracks unit types, counts, rates, promotions.
- **Upload review queue** — founder approval before data goes live.

### 9. The customer's own dashboard
- **Results dashboard** — 90-day leads/move-ins and a monthly goal tracker.
- **Campaign performance** — spend, leads, move-ins, cost-per-lead, ROAS.
- **Reports & occupancy** — snapshot, trend chart, unit mix, downloadable report.
- **Reputation view** — Google rating, review count, recent reviews.
- **Upload reports** — drag-and-drop management reports.
- **Two-way messaging** — chat thread with the team.
- **Billing & invoices** — view invoices, update payment via Stripe.
- **Setup wizard** — a 6-step onboarding that saves and resumes.
- **Mobile app feel** — installable, offline-capable, bottom tabs, pull-to-refresh.

### 10. Partner / reseller program
- **Multi-facility overview** — totals across every managed facility.
- **Add & manage facilities** — drill into occupancy, leads, campaign history.
- **Team management** — invite teammates with roles.
- **Revenue-share program** — tiers (Bronze→Platinum, 20–35%), referrals, payouts.
- **Referral engine** — codes that auto-issue credits and milestone bonuses.
- **White-label branding** — partner's own name, colors, and logo.
- **Developer tools** — API keys and webhooks for integrations.
- **Audit log & changelog** — security activity log and product updates.

### 11. Billing, signup & accounts
- **Plans & checkout** — per-facility tiers, 14-day trial, via Stripe.
- **Subscription syncing** — keeps status (trial/active/past-due/canceled) current.
- **Invoices** — founder-issued (ad spend + fee) that clients pay.
- **Usage limits & feature gates** — plan-based caps and unlocks.
- **Security** — two-factor login, session management, password reset.
- **Privacy & data deletion** — public GDPR/CCPA request flow + Facebook callback.

### 12. Messages, reminders & tracking
- **Templated emails** — branded sales/service emails (follow-up, audit, proposal, win-back).
- **White-label emails** — carry a partner's branding.
- **Text messages** — outbound SMS with quiet-hours and opt-out rules.
- **Push notifications** — browser/phone alerts to customers and partners.
- **Notification bell & preferences** — in-app alert center with per-type settings.
- **Call tracking** — dedicated numbers per page that log/attribute calls.
- **Walk-in attribution** — a counter form: "how did you hear about us?"
- **Link & visit tracking** — trackable links and page-interaction data.

> **Running quietly in the background:** about two dozen automated jobs run on a schedule with nobody pressing a button — sending the follow-ups and weekly reports, re-scoring churn and rate sensitivity, syncing Google listings and ad audiences, building NOI reports, processing uploads, and cleaning up old data.
>
> **Outside helpers it plugs into:** Claude AI (writing), Stripe (payments), Resend (email), Google (lookup & listings), Facebook / Google / TikTok (ads), AI video/image services, Cal.com (bookings), and Sentry (error alerts).

## Part 2 · Not built yet — New Ideas That Build On What Exists

Proposed features — **none of these are live**. Each one reuses machinery StorageAds already has, so it's a smaller lift than starting cold.

**How to read this:** every idea names what it *builds on* — the existing feature(s) it leans on. Tags: **[IDEA]** proposed capability · **[$$]** also a new way to make money.

### A. Smarter lead-getting  `[IDEA]`

- **Audit-to-proposal** — one click turns a finished audit into a branded pricing proposal using the facility's own revenue-loss numbers. _Builds on: audit + revenue-loss analyzer + email templates_

### B. Instant lead response  `[IDEA]`
- **Speed-to-lead auto-text** — fire a personalized SMS within seconds of a lead arriving. _Builds on: lead capture + text messages_
- **AI follow-up rep** — auto-draft (or send) the first reply per lead, tailored to their facility's biggest issue. _Builds on: lead scoring + nurture sequences + Claude_
- **Missed-call rescue** — auto-text anyone whose tracked call went unanswered, with a booking link. _Builds on: call tracking + SMS_
- **Voicemail-to-lead** — transcribe missed-call voicemails and create a scored lead automatically. _Builds on: call tracking + lead scoring_

### C. Self-optimizing ads  `[IDEA]`

### D. Pricing & revenue autopilot  `[$$]`

- **Rate-increase letter machine** — turn ECRI findings into ready-to-send, scheduled tenant rate-increase letters. _Builds on: ECRI finder + safety scoring + email/SMS_
- **What-if NOI simulator** — sliders for occupancy, rate, and ad budget that show the live impact on profit. _Builds on: NOI report + revenue analytics_

### E. Acquisition & growth intel  `[IDEA]`
- **Acquisition scout** — use market data to flag nearby facilities that look underperforming or underpriced — buy candidates. _Builds on: market scan + audit scoring_
- **Portfolio benchmarking** — rank your own facilities against each other and against the local market. _Builds on: performance aggregator + market intel_
- **New-facility demand map** — a heatmap of unmet storage demand to guide where to build or buy. _Builds on: competitor + demographics scan_
- **Lender / board NOI deck** — auto-generate an investor-ready performance deck per facility. _Builds on: NOI report + pitch voice_

### F. Reputation autopilot  `[IDEA]`
- **Feedback triage funnel** — route happy move-ins to leave a Google review and unhappy ones to a private feedback form first. _Builds on: review requests + messaging_
- **Local rank tracker** — track each facility's map-pack ranking for "storage near me" over time. _Builds on: Google listing + market scan_
- **Auto-photo refresh** — push fresh generated or uploaded photos to the Google listing on a schedule. _Builds on: image generator + listing sync + photo-refresh job_

### G. Communication hub  `[IDEA]`
- **Unified inbox** — calls, texts, Facebook messages, and portal chats in one thread per contact. _Builds on: call tracking + SMS + messaging_
- **AI texting concierge** — answer renter questions ("got a 10×10?") by text with real availability and a reservation link. _Builds on: SMS + live unit availability + reservation widget_
- **Portfolio AI assistant** — just ask "how are my ads doing this month?" and get a real answer. _Builds on: all analytics + Claude_

### H. Retention & new revenue  `[$$]`

- **Tenant-insurance revenue share** — sell tenant protection and book the upsell plus commission. _Builds on: upsell finder_
- **Performance / NOI-share pricing** — an optional tier where the fee is a share of proven profit lift instead of flat. _Builds on: NOI report (already proves the lift)_
- **Win-back autopilot upgrade** — auto-trigger a tailored offer the day a tenant moves out, by reason. _Builds on: move-out win-back + creative generator_

### I. Connect the data  `[IDEA]`
- **Live PMS sync** — a real two-way storEDGE connection so data flows automatically instead of by upload (the big one). _Builds on: PMS import + facility records_
- **Rate write-back** — push approved rate changes from StorageAds back into the PMS. _Builds on: pricing autopilot + PMS sync_
- **Move-in reconciliation** — auto-match ad-attributed move-ins against PMS move-ins to make ROI airtight. _Builds on: attribution + PMS revenue import_

### J. Partner growth  `[$$]`

- **A/B testing studio** — test two landing pages or ad creatives head-to-head and auto-pick the winner. _Builds on: landing builder + attribution (A/B is already a plan feature-gate)_

> **A note on sequencing:** the ideas that unlock the most without much new plumbing are the ones riding on data you already collect — **Live PMS sync** turns nearly every "intelligence" feature from estimate into fact, and **Budget autopilot** / **Street-rate autopilot** convert existing read-only insights into automatic action. The `[$$]`-tagged items are also new revenue lines, not just features.

## Part 3 · Frontier · Way beyond — The Most Advanced Ideas

Moonshots and platform plays. The thread running through all of them: StorageAds doesn't just market facilities — it sits on the richest live dataset in self-storage (occupancy, true street rates, competitor pricing, demand signals, attributed move-ins, tenant behavior) across many facilities and markets. That data, plus AI agents on top of it, is the real asset. These ideas turn the product into a platform, a marketplace, a data business, and eventually an operating system for the whole asset class.

**Tiers:** `[NEAR]` plausible in ~12 mo · `[BIG]` ambitious platform play · `[MOON]` moonshot / needs scale · `[$$$]` major new revenue / data sale

> **Why this matters:** marketing software is a feature; a **data network** with autonomous agents is a category. Most ideas below only work once StorageAds runs enough facilities that its aggregate data is more valuable than any single operator's. That's the flywheel: more facilities → better data → smarter automation → better results → more facilities. Build for that.

### 1. The Storage Data Network
- **Storage Price Index** `[BIG]` `[$$$]` — a "Case-Shiller for self-storage": a real-time street-rate & occupancy index per market, built from live data nobody else has. _From: competitor scrapes + PMS rates across the portfolio_
- **Alt-data feed for Wall Street** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — license anonymized storage demand & pricing signals to REIT analysts, hedge funds, and lenders as leading-indicator alt-data. _From: aggregate move-in / click / rate data_
- **Benchmark-as-a-service** `[NEAR]` `[$$$]` — any facility (even non-customers) pays to see exactly how its rates, occupancy, and reviews rank against its true local set. _From: performance aggregator + market scan_
- **Privacy-safe data co-op** `[BIG]` — facilities contribute their numbers and get back aggregate market truth they can't see alone; the network compounds. _From: PMS imports across many facilities_
- **Migration / move signal exchange** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — "people are moving here" is gold to movers, insurers, realtors, utilities; sell the anonymized demand signal downstream. _From: lead + move-in geography_

### 2. The Demand Exchange
- **Overflow lead routing** `[BIG]` `[$$$]` — when one facility is full, auto-sell its renter lead to a nearby facility with vacancy; a live lead marketplace. _From: live availability + lead capture + attribution_
- **Programmatic lead bidding** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — facilities bid in real time for renter intent by unit size, distance, and price. _From: lead marketplace + auction engine_
- **Reverse marketplace** `[BIG]` — renters post "need a 10×10 near downtown under $120"; facilities auto-respond with offers. _From: reservation widget + availability + pricing_
- **Network occupancy balancing** `[MOON]` — steer demand across a portfolio (or the whole network) to maximize total NOI, like a hotel revenue network. _From: demand exchange + pricing autopilot_

### 3. "Stripe for Storage" — dev platform
- **Storage API / SDK** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — let any app (realtor sites, moving companies, apartment portals) sell or reserve storage through StorageAds and take a cut. _From: v1 API + availability + reservation_
- **Embeddable widgets** `[NEAR]` — drop-in audit, live-availability, review, and booking widgets for any facility's own website. _From: audit tool + availability API + GBP_
- **Plugin / app marketplace** `[BIG]` `[$$$]` — third parties build and sell add-ons on top of the platform; revenue share. _From: v1 API + webhooks + partner orgs_
- **AI-agent interface (MCP)** `[NEAR]` — expose a facility's marketing/occupancy state so external AI agents (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) can query and act on it. _From: v1 API + structured data_
- **Native PMS-marketplace apps** `[BIG]` — ship StorageAds as an installable app inside storEDGE, Yardi, and other PMS app stores. _From: live PMS sync + creative engine_

### 4. Autonomous AI operators
- **The Autonomous Operator** `[MOON]` — give it a goal ("hold 92% at max NOI") and a budget; it writes, launches, prices, optimizes, and reports with zero clicks. _From: every automation feature, coordinated by an agent_
- **AI revenue manager** `[BIG]` — airline-style dynamic pricing that reprices every unit type continuously. _From: pricing autopilot + occupancy + competitor data_
- **Facility digital twin** `[MOON]` — a simulator to test "what if I cut rates 5% / double ad spend" before risking a dollar. _From: forecast + NOI model + reinforcement learning_
- **AI retention negotiator** `[BIG]` — a text/chat agent that detects a leaving tenant and negotiates a personalized save offer in real time. _From: churn prediction + messaging + Claude_
- **24/7 AI voice receptionist** `[BIG]` `[$$$]` — answers every call, quotes real availability, books tours, and takes reservations day or night. _From: call tracking + availability + reservation_
- **Multi-agent operator brain** `[MOON]` — pricing, marketing, and reputation agents that negotiate with each other toward one NOI objective. _From: all of the above orchestrated_

### 5. Embedded finance & fintech
- **The Storage NOI Score** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — a verified, real-time creditworthiness primitive for a facility, used to underwrite lending — owned by StorageAds. _From: NOI report + PMS sync (verified financials)_
- **Revenue-based financing** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — advance operators capital against future move-ins, underwritten by data StorageAds already trusts. _From: NOI score + attribution_
- **Embedded tenant insurance** `[NEAR]` `[$$$]` — broker or underwrite tenant protection at the point of reservation; recurring commission. _From: upsell finder + reservation flow_
- **Payments & rent financing** `[BIG]` `[$$$]` — process tenant payments and offer BNPL on storage; take the spread. _From: Stripe rails + tenant CRM_

### 6. Acquisition & capital markets
- **Auto-underwriting buy-box** `[BIG]` — point it at a facility for sale and it underwrites the deal and drafts an LOI from real market data. _From: acquisition scout + NOI model + market scan_
- **Deal marketplace** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — connect buyers and sellers of facilities, with StorageAds-verified performance as the listing. _From: portfolio data + valuation API_
- **Appraisal-grade valuation API** `[BIG]` `[$$$]` — sell instant, defensible facility valuations to lenders, brokers, and appraisers. _From: data network + NOI model_
- **Fractional / syndication platform** `[MOON]` — let investors back facilities, with live verified performance as the prospectus. _From: valuation + NOI reporting + portal_

### 7. Frontier advertising & reality
- **Occupancy-triggered billboards** `[BIG]` — programmatic digital out-of-home near the facility that switches on only when units sit empty. _From: occupancy data + ad publisher (DOOH)_
- **Connected-TV & streaming buys** `[NEAR]` — extend the ad engine into CTV/streaming, hyper-targeted by trade area. _From: creative generator + audience sync_
- **Life-event geofencing** `[BIG]` — trigger ads when someone rents a U-Haul, tours an apartment, or lists a home nearby. _From: signal intelligence + ad publisher_
- **AR "see your unit"** `[MOON]` — let a renter walk a unit in augmented reality from the ad before reserving. _From: facility assets + reservation_
- **Infinite personalized video** `[BIG]` — a unique generated video ad per audience micro-segment, at scale. _From: AI video generator + audience data_

### 8. Predictive signal intelligence
- **Life-event demand radar** `[MOON]` — mine apartment turnover, building permits, business closures, estate sales, divorce/probate filings into a forward demand forecast per block. _From: scrapers + market scan + forecasting_
- **Household move-propensity model** `[MOON]` — score who in a trade area is likely to need storage soon, and target them. _From: demographics + life-event signals_
- **Pre-emptive churn save** `[BIG]` — act on a tenant's leaving signal before they consciously decide to go. _From: churn scoring + behavioral signals_
- **Macro & weather demand model** `[NEAR]` — fold interest rates, housing turnover, and weather into the occupancy forecast. _From: forecast engine + external data_

### 9. Physical layer — IoT & robotics
- **Smart-lock & sensor sync** `[BIG]` — read true unit occupancy from door sensors and smart locks instead of trusting the spreadsheet. _From: facility records + new IoT integration_
- **Computer-vision site audits** `[MOON]` — drone or camera sweeps that read which units are actually occupied and flag curb-appeal issues. _From: site-map vision reader, extended to live imagery_
- **Auto-listing of freed units** `[BIG]` — the instant a sensor sees a vacate, the unit hits ads, the listing, and the demand exchange. _From: IoT + publisher + demand exchange_
- **Dynamic on-site signage** `[MOON]` — screens at the gate that change pricing/promos based on live occupancy. _From: pricing autopilot + DOOH_

### 10. Beyond storage — Local Occupancy OS
- **Adjacent verticals** `[BIG]` — the same engine runs RV/boat storage, parking, marinas, laundromats, car washes, mobile-home parks — any local, occupancy-driven business. _From: the whole stack, re-skinned_
- **"Occupancy OS" platform** `[MOON]` — generalize into the marketing + revenue brain for any fragmented local asset class. _From: vertical generalization_
- **Geographic / franchise licensing** `[BIG]` `[$$$]` — license the operating system to REITs and operators in new regions as their internal marketing OS. _From: white-label + full platform_

### 11. Ad-hoc data store & syndication
- **Self-serve data API store** `[NEAR]` `[$$$]` — pay-per-query access to competitor pricing, occupancy, and demand by market; metered like an API product. _From: data network + v1 API + usage metering_
- **On-demand market reports** `[NEAR]` `[$$$]` — auto-generate and sell a polished market study for any metro, instantly. _From: market scan + NOI/report engine_
- **Investor research subscription** `[BIG]` `[$$$]` — a recurring data product for storage investors, brokers, and lenders. _From: Storage Price Index + benchmarks_
- **Consumer-intent syndication** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — supply anonymized renter-intent signals to ad networks and adjacent advertisers. _From: lead + interaction data (privacy-governed)_

### 12. Moonshots & wild cards
- **Become the system of record** `[MOON]` — move up the stack from marketing layer to full PMS replacement and own the whole operator relationship. _From: PMS sync, inverted_
- **StorageAds Capital** `[MOON]` `[$$$]` — buy underperforming facilities the data flags, fix them with the platform, and keep the upside. _From: acquisition scout + autonomous operator_
- **Industry-standard rating** `[MOON]` — make the StorageAds score the "FICO of storage facilities" that lenders and buyers require. _From: NOI score + data network_
- **ESG / carbon reporting** `[NEAR]` — auto-produce sustainability reporting facilities increasingly need for financing. _From: facility records + report engine_
- **Tokenized / fractional units** `[MOON]` — explore on-chain fractional ownership of storage income streams (skeptically — regulatory minefield). _From: valuation + syndication platform_

> **The one-sentence thesis:** StorageAds' durable moat isn't the ad tooling — it's becoming the **data and demand layer** for self-storage, then renting that position out as APIs, a marketplace, financing, and alt-data. Pick the wedge (likely the `[NEAR]` data-benchmark + embeddable widgets), use it to pull more facilities onto the network, and let the flywheel make every `[MOON]` idea progressively cheaper to reach.
