# Go-To-Market Strategy: StorageAds.com

## Executive Summary

Target 3-15 facility owner-operators in secondary markets with overbuilt conditions, $500-3,000/mo marketing spend, no attribution, occupancy 75-90%. Start in Sun Belt (Atlanta, Orlando, Phoenix, Tampa, Dallas). Use free audit tool as top-of-funnel, blend product-led and direct sales, lean on operator credibility and proprietary benchmarks.

Expected timeline: 30 days to first paying customer post-alpha, 90 days to 5-10 customers, 12 months to 25-50 customers.

---

## TARGET SEGMENT (Beachhead Market)

### Ideal Customer Profile

**Operator Type:**
- Owner-operator or small management company (3-15 facilities)
- Independent (not REIT-backed)
- Actively involved in marketing decisions (not fully delegated to a generic marketing team)
- Tech-comfortable but not tech-focused (understands ROI, can follow dashboards)

**Current State:**
- Spending $500-3,000/mo on marketing (too big to ignore, small enough to feel pain)
- Running ads but with zero attribution (using Facebook/Google ad manager, getting clicks, guessing at ROI)
- Occupancy 75-90% (not desperate, but optimizing for the last 10-15%)
- Using storEDGE or SiteLink (makes PMS integration seamless)
- Never worked with a dedicated marketing team before (less vendor cynicism than large operators)

**Geographic Focus:**
- Overbuilt Sun Belt markets: Atlanta, Orlando, Phoenix, Tampa, Dallas, Austin, Nashville, Charlotte, Raleigh
- Secondary markets in TX, FL, GA, NC, TN (not NYC or LA — those are consolidator markets)
- Markets with 50-150 facilities within 20 miles (enough competition to drive CAC awareness)

**Why this segment:**
- Biggest pain point: "I'm spending money on ads and have no idea if they're working"
- Highest motivation: "If I could cut my cost per move-in by 30%, that's $X per month in profit"
- Most underserved: Agencies ignore them (too small), Adverank targets larger operators, G5 ignores them entirely
- Best buyers: Operators are used to ROI metrics and performance data (easier to sell to than other industries)

## CUSTOMER ACQUISITION CHANNELS (Ranked by Expected ROI)

### Channel 1: Free Audit Tool → Demo → Close (Highest Confidence)

**Mechanism:**
1. Operator visits /audit-tool, enters facility info
2. System generates marketing diagnostic: "You're spending $X on ads, generating Y leads, cost per move-in is $Z"
3. "Schedule a call to review results and see what's possible"
4. Sales call with Blake: show personalized improvement roadmap based on their data + Blake's benchmarks
5. Demo of StorageAds platform + Blake's own facility results as proof
6. Close: "Let's run this on your building for 30 days"

**Why this works:**
- Operator immediately sees their own performance gaps (visceral "I didn't realize I was paying that much per move-in")
- Blake credibility is baked in ("I'm running this on my facilities")
- Low friction to entry (free, quick, relevant)
- Self-qualifying (only serious operators complete the audit)

**Metrics to track:**
- Audit completions per week
- Demo call conversion (% of audits that book a call)
- Call-to-pilot conversion (% of demos that become 30-day pilots)
- Pilot-to-paid conversion (% of pilots that convert to customers)

**Target:** 15-20 audit completions/week by end of month 1.

### Channel 4: Direct Outreach to Target Operators

**Mechanism:**
1. Find operators in target markets via Google Maps (search "self storage" in Atlanta, etc.)
2. Identify those with storEDGE or SiteLink websites (lower friction to integration)
3. Find owner contact info (LinkedIn, facility websites, SSA directories)
4. Send personalized email: "I noticed your facility in [market]. Running any digital marketing? I've built something specifically for operators like you."
5. If no response, follow up with audit tool link ("Here's a free diagnostic of your marketing")
6. If still no response, try LinkedIn message

**Why this works:**
- Highly targeted (you're only reaching operators in priority markets with the right PMS)
- Personal (reference their specific facility)
- Low friction offer (free audit)
- Self-qualifying (only operators who care about marketing will respond)

**Metrics to track:**
- Outreach volume per week
- Response rate (% of emails/messages that get replies)
- Audit completion rate (% of responses that convert to audit)
- Demo call rate (% of audits that convert to calls)

**Sample Outreach Email:**
"Hi [Name], I noticed [Facility Name] in [City]. I'm Blake, an operator running [number of facilities]. I've spent the last year building StorageAds — a platform that tracks whether your marketing ads actually drive move-ins (most operators can't see this). Would you be open to a quick marketing diagnostic for your facility? Takes 5 minutes: [link to audit tool]"

**Target:** 50 outreach per week by end of month 1. 5-10% response rate. 20-30% of responses complete audit.
