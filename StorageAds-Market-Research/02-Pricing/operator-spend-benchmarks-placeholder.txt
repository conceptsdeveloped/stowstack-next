This file will be developed as an Excel/Sheets file to compile operator marketing spend data.

PLANNED STRUCTURE:

Sheet 1: Spend Benchmarks by Facility Size
- Facility size (sq ft): 5k, 10k, 25k, 50k, 100k+
- Average monthly ad spend
- Average monthly platform fees (SaaS vs. agency)
- Total marketing spend (fees + ad spend)
- % of revenue allocated to marketing

Sheet 2: Spend by Operator Type
- Single-facility independent operators
- Small chains (2-5 facilities)
- Regional chains (6-20 facilities)
- Enterprise portfolios (20+)
- Management companies (white-label)

For each:
- Typical monthly ad spend
- Typical platform/agency fees
- Total marketing budget
- Channels invested in (Meta, Google, SEO, email, landing pages, etc.)

Sheet 3: ROI Benchmarks
- Average cost-per-lead (all channels combined)
- Average cost-per-rental (by channel)
- Average revenue per rental (by facility type)
- Implied marketing ROI

Sheet 4: Pricing Tier Breakdown
- Tier: Budget/DIY, Mid-market SaaS, Mid-market Agency, Premium, Enterprise
- % of operators in each tier
- Average monthly spend in each tier
- Typical facility count per tier

Sheet 5: Geographic & Market Variation
- Hot markets (high competition, high spend): e.g., Austin, Phoenix, Denver
- Moderate markets: Typical mid-US cities
- Cold markets (low competition, low spend): Rural areas, declining markets

For each:
- Average ad spend per facility
- Average platform fees
- Typical ad spend allocation (% Meta, % Google, % SpareFoot, etc.)

DATA SOURCES:
- StorageAds customer data (actual spend)
- Public API integrations (Meta ads library, Google transparency data)
- Industry reports (SSA, SELFSTORAGE.com)
- Competitive pricing pages
- Interviews with operators and agencies

STATUS:
To be built in Google Sheets and exported to .xlsx format.
Will be updated quarterly as new customer data becomes available.
