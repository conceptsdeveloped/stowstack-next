# Pricing Landscape — Storage Marketing Solutions

## Executive Summary

Storage marketing pricing spans five distinct tiers: DIY tools ($29-80/mo), mid-market SaaS ($245-899/mo), agency retainers ($1,000-3,000/mo), full-service agencies ($2,000-5,000/mo), and enterprise solutions ($5,000+/mo). StorageAds at $750-1,500/mo (Product A) and $2,000-2,500/mo (bundle) sits in the upper mid-market, competing directly with premium SaaS and smaller full-service agencies.

## Pure-Play Storage Marketing Solutions

### StorageRankers
**Type:** SaaS platform (SEO-focused)
**Pricing Model:** Tiered subscription, no long-term contracts
**Price Points:**
- Starter: $299/mo
- Elite: $799/mo
- Setup fee: $599-999 (one-time)

**What's Included:** SEO optimization, rank tracking, content management, technical on-page fixes
**What's NOT:** PPC, social ads, landing pages, call tracking, attribution
**Minimum Commitment:** Month-to-month, cancel anytime
**Status:** Confirmed — public website

**Notes:** Pure SEO play. No ad spend management or performance attribution. Primarily for organic search visibility.

## Performance-Based / Co-Op Models

### SpareFoot
**Type:** Performance-based lead aggregator
**Pricing Model:** Bid-based cost-per-rental
**Price Range:** Typically 1.5-3x monthly rent per move-in

**Example:** $50/mo unit = $75-150 fee per rental (typical)
**Effective Monthly Cost:** Highly variable; $500-2,000+/mo for active facilities

**What's Included:** Lead generation, customer match, performance-based payment
**What's NOT:** Marketing strategy, ongoing optimization, attribution beyond lead source
**Minimum Commitment:** Month-to-month (pay per lead)
**Status:** Confirmed — public model

**Notes:** Pay-for-performance reduces upfront risk but can become expensive at scale. No monthly retainer; cost is tied to rental demand and bid strategy.

## Key Insights

**Entry point:** Adverank Cloud at $29/mo is the lowest cost of entry for active ad management; Storelocal at $80/mo for lead generation alternative.

**Mid-market clustering:** StoragePug ($245), Storagely ($245), and StorageRankers Starter ($299) cluster around $245-300/mo. This is the "affordable marketing tool" anchor for single-facility operators.

**Agency jump:** Gap between $800/mo (highest SaaS tier) and $1,000+/mo (agency minimum) is significant. Operators must choose: tool-only or full-service.

**Premium agency range:** Blue Corona, Thrive, and comparable shops cluster $2,000-5,000/mo. These are the reference point for "what full-service costs."

**Performance-based advantage:** SpareFoot's 1.5-3x rent model can be cheaper than retainers for low-demand properties but scales quickly.

**Enterprise complexity:** G5 and RealPage pricing is entirely bespoke. Large portfolios (20+ facilities) negotiate heavily; no two deals are identical.
